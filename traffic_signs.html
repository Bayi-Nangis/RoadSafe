    • Interactive Traffic Signs

    Interactive cards to help you understand traffic signs.

    Traffic Light

    Red Traffic Light

    Meaning

    Vehicles must come to a complete stop behind the stop line (zebra crossing). You are prohibited from proceeding.

    Traffic Light

    Yellow Traffic Light

    Meaning

    Vehicles must be prepared to stop or proceed with extreme caution. When this light is on, you must not accelerate your vehicle.

    Traffic Light

    Green Traffic Light

    Meaning

    Vehicles may proceed, but must do so with caution.

    Prohibition

    Prohibition of Entry

    Meaning

    Entry is prohibited for motor vehicles and non-motor vehicles.

    Command

    Roundabout

    Meaning

    Vehicles must follow the direction indicated when entering the roundabout.

    Command

    Minimum Speed

    Meaning

    The minimum speed required for vehicles, in this example 60 km/jam.

    Alignment

    Narrow Road

    Meaning

    The road ahead becomes narrower, and drivers must be prepared to reduce speed and exercise caution.

    Prohibition

    No Stopping

    Meaning

    Vehicles must not stop in the area indicated by the sign.

    Prohibition

    Keep Distance

    Meaning

    Vehicles must keep distance between themselves and the vehicle ahead, for example by 60 meters.

    Prohibition

    Prohibition of U-Turn

    Meaning

    U-turns are prohibited in the area indicated by the sign.

    Prohibition

    Maximum Speed

    Meaning

    The maximum speed allowed for vehicles in the area indicated by the sign, for example 80 km per hour.

    Command

    Bus Only

    Meaning

    Only buses are allowed to use this lane or road.
